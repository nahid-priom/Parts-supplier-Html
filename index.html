Welcome To Moshjid-E-Bilal

We are a Muslim community organization providing various services to our fellow Muslim brothers and sisters. We offer the opportunity to attend prayer 5 times and other facilities.

Our Mission

To nurture a community dedicated to faith, unity, and service, while offering prayer facilities and educational programs to help members grow spiritually.

Our Vision

To create a welcoming space for all Muslims where they can strengthen their faith, serve the community, and foster the next generation of leaders through Islamic education.

Follow The Path Of Islam

Teaching Quran

Join us in learning the Quran, understanding its teachings, and implementing its wisdom in daily life.

Umrah & Hajj

Join us in organizing spiritual journeys for Umrah and Hajj, and experience the pilgrimage.

Community Services

We provide support and services to strengthen and help our Muslim community.

Funeral Services

We offer funeral services and support for families in their time of need.
